      <li><strong>401:</strong> Unauthorized (invalid API key)</li>
      <li><strong>429:</strong> Rate limit exceeded</li>
      <li><strong>500:</strong> Server error</li>

## Models and Capabilities

<div className="space-y-4">
  <div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg p-6">
    <h3 className="text-lg font-bold mb-2">Which AI models does All AI offer?</h3>
    <p>All AI provides access to a variety of models for different purposes:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc list-inside mt-2 space-y-1">
      <li><strong>Text models:</strong> AllText Pro (200B parameters), AllText Lite (7B parameters)</li>
      <li><strong>Code models:</strong> AllCode (specialized for programming)</li>
      <li><strong>Vision models:</strong> AllVision (image analysis), AllImage (image generation)</li>
      <li><strong>Audio models:</strong> AllSpeech (speech recognition), AllVoice (text-to-speech), AllSonic (audio generation)</li>
    </ul>
    <p className="mt-2">For a complete list with detailed descriptions, see our <a href="/models" className="text-blue-500 hover:text-blue-600">Models</a> page.</p>
  </div>

  <div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg p-6">
    <h3 className="text-lg font-bold mb-2">How do I choose the right model for my use case?</h3>
    <p>When selecting a model, consider factors such as:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc list-inside mt-2 space-y-1">
      <li><strong>Task complexity:</strong> More complex tasks may require larger models</li>
      <li><strong>Latency requirements:</strong> If speed is critical, smaller models might be preferable</li>
      <li><strong>Cost considerations:</strong> Larger models generally cost more to use</li>
      <li><strong>Specialization:</strong> Domain-specific models often perform better for specific tasks</li>
    </ul>
    <p className="mt-2">Our <a href="/tools/model-selector" className="text-blue-500 hover:text-blue-600">interactive model selector</a> can help you find the right model based on your specific requirements.</p>
  </div>

  <div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg p-6">
    <h3 className="text-lg font-bold mb-2">How do I control the output of text generation models?</h3>
    <p>You can influence the output of our text models using various parameters:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc list-inside mt-2 space-y-1">
      <li><strong>Temperature:</strong> Controls randomness (0.0-2.0, lower = more deterministic)</li>
      <li><strong>Top-p:</strong> Controls diversity via nucleus sampling (0.0-1.0)</li>
      <li><strong>Frequency penalty:</strong> Reduces repetition of token sequences (-2.0-2.0)</li>
      <li><strong>Presence penalty:</strong> Reduces repetition of topics (-2.0-2.0)</li>
      <li><strong>Stop sequences:</strong> Defines where the model should stop generating</li>
    </ul>
    <p className="mt-2">For more details on these parameters, see our <a href="/guides/text-generation" className="text-blue-500 hover:text-blue-600">Text Generation Guide</a>.</p>
  </div>

  <div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg p-6">
    <h3 className="text-lg font-bold mb-2">Are my inputs and outputs stored by All AI?</h3>
    <p>By default, All AI does not permanently store the content of your API requests or responses for privacy and security reasons. However, we may temporarily store this information for debugging purposes, typically for no more than 30 days.</p>
    <p className="mt-2">If you're using our Enterprise plan, you can enable our data retention feature to store your inputs and outputs for model fine-tuning or to maintain an audit trail of AI-generated content.</p>
    <p className="mt-2">For more information, please review our <a href="https://allai.com/privacy" className="text-blue-500 hover:text-blue-600">Privacy Policy</a>.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Billing and Pricing

<div className="space-y-4">
  <div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg p-6">
    <h3 className="text-lg font-bold mb-2">How is usage billed?</h3>
    <p>All AI uses a token-based billing system. Tokens are units of text that our models process, with approximately 4 characters per token for English text. You're billed based on the total number of tokens processed, which includes both input (prompt) and output (completion) tokens.</p>
    <p className="mt-2">Different models have different token rates. For example:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc list-inside mt-2 space-y-1">
      <li>AllText Pro: $0.01 per 1,000 tokens</li>
      <li>AllText Lite: $0.002 per 1,000 tokens</li>
      <li>AllCode: $0.015 per 1,000 tokens</li>
    </ul>
    <p className="mt-2">Image and audio models have separate pricing models based on resolution, duration, and other factors. Visit our <a href="https://allai.com/pricing" className="text-blue-500 hover:text-blue-600">Pricing</a> page for detailed information.</p>
  </div>
